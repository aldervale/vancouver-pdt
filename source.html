The Evening Win
In winter, sunset shifts from ~4:15 PM (old PST) to ~5:15 PM permanent PDT. That means an extra hour of usable daylight after work and school — huge for outdoor activities, commuting safety, and mood.
The Morning Trade-off
Sunrise in late December won't arrive until ~8:56 AM — nearly two hours after a 7 AM wake-up. Kids heading to school and early commuters will start the day in full darkness for about 3 months (Nov–Jan).
